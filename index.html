About Us

A Community United by the Love of Libraries

In April 2003, a group of passionate local book enthusiasts came together with a shared vision: to strengthen and celebrate Anoka County Library. From those humble beginnings, the Friends have grown into an essential force supporting one of the county's most beloved and vital public resources.

As an all-volunteer organization, every member, donor, and volunteer makes a direct difference. We work alongside library staff to fund special programs, host events, and ensure that every resident of Anoka County has access to an inspiring, well-resourced library experience.

The Friends of the Anoka County Library raises funds for and increases awareness of Anoka County Library in its mission to connect community to ideas, information, and inspiration.

What We Do

  • Host annual fundraising galas and book sales
  • Fund library programs and special events
  • Support the Summer and Winter Reading Programs
  • Fund the Book Club-in-a-Bag collection
  • Sponsor the Reading Bridge program for struggling readers
  • Promote Anoka County libraries countywide
  • Advocate for the library's role in the community
  • Provide resources that bridge funding gaps

Ways to Help

More Ways to Support the Libraries

Not sure how to help beyond donating or volunteering? Here are some meaningful ways anyone can make a difference for Anoka County libraries and the communities they serve.

📖

Donate Books

Gently used books in good condition can be donated to the Friends for use in our book sales. Contact us to learn about accepted items and drop-off locations.

🗣

Spread the Word

Tell your neighbors, friends, and coworkers about the Friends and the library. Follow and share our social media posts to help us reach more of the community.

🏢

Corporate Sponsorship

Businesses can sponsor events like our gala, donate auction items, or partner on programs. It's a great way to give back while raising your community profile.

🎁

Shop for the Library

Use AmazonSmile or other charitable shopping programs to designate Friends of the Anoka County Library as your beneficiary at no extra cost to you.

📝

Write a Letter

Advocate for library funding by writing letters to county commissioners and local officials. Showing community support helps secure vital public funding.

🎓

Honor or Memorial Gifts

Celebrate a loved one by making a gift in their honor or memory. These meaningful contributions can fund books, programs, or specific library needs.

👵

Legacy Giving

Consider including the Friends in your estate planning. A legacy gift ensures the library remains a vibrant community hub for generations to come.

🌟

Refer a Friend

Bring a friend to a meeting, book sale, or gala! Growing our membership and volunteer base multiplies our impact across all of Anoka County.

Leadership & Meetings

Help Lead the Friends

Becoming a Friends board member is a meaningful way to serve your community and have a direct hand in shaping the future of Anoka County Library. Board members help organize events, develop programs, and grow public support for the library system.

Why Join the Board?

  • Build leadership experience in your community
  • Work directly with library staff and administration
  • Organize events, programs, and fundraising campaigns
  • Connect with fellow book lovers and community leaders
  • Make a lasting difference for Anoka County residents
  • Shape how the Friends grows and evolves over time
